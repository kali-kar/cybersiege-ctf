// Competition Rules

Rules & Guidelines

⚙️ General Conduct
  • Pre-registration is mandatory before the event
  • Teams of 1–4 members; one participant per team only
  • Open to all branches & years of study
  • Respectful, sportsmanlike behaviour at all times
  • Min. 50% team presence on-site throughout
🏆 Scoring System
  • Flag format: CS{...}
  • Points awarded on first correct flag submission
  • First Blood bonus on each challenge
  • Hints available but may deduct points
  • Tie-breaker: earliest last successful solve
🚫 Prohibited Actions
  • No attacking competition infrastructure or other teams
  • No sharing of flags or solutions during the event
  • No brute-forcing the CTF platform
  • No scoreboard manipulation of any kind
  • Violations result in immediate disqualification
📜 Certificates
  • Participation cert for teams solving ≥1 challenge
  • Winner certificates for Top 3 teams
  • Digital certs issued within 7 days post-event
  • NAAC A+ institution — certs hold academic value
